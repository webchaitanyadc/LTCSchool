Our Story

LEC Smart Educational Institute was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to revolutionize education by empowering students with time, technology, and personalized attention.

Founded in Jalna, Maharashtra, we recognized a critical gap in the Indian education system. Traditional schooling was overloading students, leaving them with barely 30% of their time for the deep, self-directed learning essential for competitive exam success.

We asked ourselves: "What if we could flip the model?"

This question led to the creation of our innovative 70/30 learning model - giving students 70% of their time back for critical thinking, problem-solving, and mastery, while providing 30% of laser-focused, high-quality instruction from India's best educators.

Partnering with NIOS (National Institute of Open Schooling), we became Jalna's first open schooling initiative, offering government-recognized certification while preparing students for premier competitive exams like IIT-JEE, NEET, and more.

Today, LEC Smart stands as a beacon of affordable, quality education that puts students first - combining cutting-edge technology, AI-driven learning, and proven pedagogical methods to create future leaders.

Our Vision

To revolutionize the education model by leveraging technology and artificial intelligence to create globally capable and skilled students through the Indian educational system.

We envision a future where every Indian student has access to world-class education that respects their time, nurtures their potential, and prepares them to compete on a global stage.

We are building a win-win ecosystem where students reclaim their time, master competitive concepts, and grow into globally competitive leaders equipped with:

  • Critical thinking abilities
  • Problem-solving skills
  • Strong conceptual foundation
  • Time management mastery
  • Self-directed learning habits

Our Mission

Maximizing Indian Youth Potential

1. Time Empowerment: To grant every student 70% of their time back for deep, meaningful self-learning, essential for acquiring critical thinking and problem-solving skills that competitive exams demand.

2. Competitive Mastery: To provide a focused, rigorous academic model aimed at ensuring success in premier entrance exams like IIT, NEET, NIT, BITSAT, IPMAT, and CA Foundation.

3. Affordable Excellence: To make quality education accessible by offering a 60% cost reduction compared to traditional school-plus-coaching models.

4. Technology Integration: To harness AI and digital tools for personalized learning experiences that adapt to each student's needs.
