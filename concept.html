Our Open Schooling Concept

Revolutionizing Education Through Open Schooling

Open schooling is a flexible and innovative approach to education that breaks away from the rigid structure of traditional schools. It empowers students to learn at their own pace while receiving world-class instruction and government-recognized certification.

At LEC Smart, we've partnered with NIOS (National Institute of Open Schooling) - a Central Government Board recognized by all universities and competitive exams across India - to offer an education model that prioritizes student success, time management, and competitive exam preparation.

The Challenge & Our Answer

Why Open Schooling?

The Problem

Traditional schooling overloads students, leaving inadequate time for deep learning.

  • Students spend 6-8 hours daily in school
  • Only 30% time remains for self-study and practice
  • Separate coaching adds 3-4 more hours
  • No time for critical thinking development
  • High costs for school + coaching
  • Physical and mental exhaustion
  • Unable to focus on competitive exam preparation

The LEC Smart Solution

Our 70/30 open schooling model flips the script entirely.

  • 70% time dedicated to self-study and practice
  • 30% laser-focused daily instruction
  • School + coaching integrated in one system
  • Time for deep learning and mastery
  • 60% cost reduction compared to traditional
  • Flexible, stress-free learning environment
  • Complete focus on competitive exam success
Our Unique Approach

The 70/30 Learning Model Explained

70%

Self-Learning & Practice

The Competitive Edge

Students get 70% of their time back for:

  • Deep practice and problem-solving
  • Concept internalization through repetition
  • Error analysis and correction
  • Mock tests and previous year papers
  • Critical thinking development
  • Self-paced learning and revision

This is the time that separates average students from toppers in competitive exams.

30%

Intensive Instruction

Maximum Efficiency

30% focused time dedicated to:

  • Live interactive classes with best faculty
  • Conceptual clarity in Math, Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Competitive exam strategies and shortcuts
  • Daily Practice Papers (DPP)
  • Doubt clearing sessions
  • Progress tracking and mentorship

Every minute of instruction is purposeful and aligned with competitive exam requirements.
