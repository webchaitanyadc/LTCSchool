Our Methodology

The Triangle of Learning

In today's competitive world, memorizing facts is not enough. Success requires completing the triangle of Knowledge, Skills, and Critical Thinking.

Step 1

Learn

Daily video classes from India's top faculties through flexible online mode with student-friendly interface and advanced learning tools.

Step 2

Evaluate

Daily Practice Papers (DPP) at the end of each session to immediately check understanding and identify knowledge gaps.

Step 3

Correct

Homework based on DPP scores, checked by parents at home, then verified by teachers the next day for complete mastery.

The Problem: Traditional schools leave only 30% time for self-learning. We need 70% self-learning time to complete the triangle.
